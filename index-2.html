KING OF SAUDI ARABIA

The king of Saudi Arabia is the monarchial head of state and head of government of Saudi Arabia who holds absolute power. He is the head of the Saudi Arabian royal family, the House of Saud.

Abdelaziz ben Abderrahmane Al Saoud

He was born in Riyadh in 1876 , the capital of the Kingdom, he united the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, and worked to improve the social and economic situation and education, and in the year 1953 he died in his palace in the Taif Governorate.

1932-1953
Saud bin Abdulaziz Al Saud

He was born in Kuwait in 1902 and took power in 1953, established the first university in Saudi Arabia, King Saud University in Riyadh, established the Islamic University in Medina, and established the first military college, King Abdulaziz Military University in Riyadh. He died in 1969 in Athens, Greece.

1953-1964
Faisal bin Abdulaziz Al Saud

He was born in Riyadh and took power in 1953, He was Chairman of the Shura Council, Minister of Foreign Affairs and Prime Minister. After the end of World War I in 1918, his father, King Abdulaziz, sent him on a visit to Britain and France. He was named Person of the Year by Time magazine in 1974. He died in 1975 in Riyadh.

1964-1975
Khaled ben Abdelaziz Al Saoud

He was born in Riyadh in 1913 and took power in 1975, During his rule, huge companies were established, such as the Saudi Basic Industries Corporation (SABIC), and six new ministries, in addition to nine administrative and scientific centers, most notably: the Royal Commission for Jubail and Yanbu. He died in 1982 in Taif.

1975-1982
Fahd bin Abdulaziz Al Saud

He was born in Riyadh in 1923 and came to power in 1982, He established a basic system of government equivalent to the constitution. Establishing the King Fahd Complex for the Printing of the Noble Qur’an; Where this copy of the Koran was widely spread in Islamic circles. Establishment of two ministries: Civil Service and Islamic Affairs. Construction of two airports: King Fahd International in Dammam, and King Khalid International in Riyadh.

1982-2005
Abdallah ben Abdelaziz Al Saoud

He was born in Riyadh in 1924 and took power in 2005. Launching the King Abdullah Scholarship Program, opening King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), establishing Jazan Economic City, establishing King Abdullah City for Atomic and Renewable Energy, starting construction works for King Abdullah Specialist Children’s Hospital in Jeddah, He died in 2015 in Riyadh.

2005-2015
Salmane ben Abdelaziz Al Saoud

He was born in Riyadh in 1935 and took power in 2015. During his life, King Salman received many honorary degrees, the most important of which are: an honorary doctorate in literature from Umm Al-Qura University in Makkah. In recognition of his charitable humanitarian efforts, in addition to his keenness to support education. King Salman assumed the presidency of the Charitable Society for Orphans Care in Riyadh, the presidency of the King Abdul Aziz Foundation, and the head of the Popular Committee for Aiding the Palestinian People.

2015-Now
