University of Tabuk

Tabuk University was established 16 years ago, i.e. in 2006. It is a public university with 4 main campuses, including 18 colleges. Its location is in the city of Tabuk, which is located in the northwestern part of the Kingdom. The most prominent programs obtained by the university are the pre-bachelor’s technical and functional education program at the Community College, from the Academic Accreditation Council accredited by the Ministry of Education in the United States of America in 2010. Mechanical Engineering program in the College of Engineering from ABET in 2016-2017. Computer Science program from the College of Computing and Information Technology from ABET in 2016-2017. Mathematics program in the Faculty of Science of ASEN in the year 2014-2015. The English Language Program at the College of Education from CEA in 2016-2017, its current director is Dr. Abdullah bin Mufreh Al Dhiyabi.
